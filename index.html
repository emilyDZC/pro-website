About

Emily is a professional violinist with a career spanning both classical and jazz performance. Trained to the highest orchestral standards, she regularly performs with ensembles including the City of Birmingham Symphony Orchestra, Opera North, Sinfonia Viva, and the Brigantes Orchestra.

Alongside her orchestral work, she brings individuality and flair to the stage as a solo performer in both classical repertoire and gypsy jazz.

Gypsy jazz ensembles

Sheffield-based ensembles

Jazz Hot Six

A high-energy gypsy jazz band based in Sheffield. We hold a monthly residency at Yellow Arch Studios, a popular and always-packed event where we bring the spirit of Django Reinhardt and Stephane Grappelli to life.


Hot Flamingo

A lively ensemble blending gypsy jazz with vocals, creating a playful and engaging performance style. With our charismatic singer out front, we bring a fun, vibrant twist to the tradition.

Classical playing

Freelance orchestral player | Performer for events and functions

Emily has played regularly with the CBSO for over 15 years, including concerts around the UK and abroad.

She also performs with orchestras around the north of England and Midlands including Opera North, Sinfonia Viva and the Brigantes Orchestra.

She is the leader of the British Philharmonic Concert Orchestra.
